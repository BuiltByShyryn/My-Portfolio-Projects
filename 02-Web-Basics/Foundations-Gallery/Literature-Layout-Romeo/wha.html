Cooking Basics

What is cooking

Cooking is the art and science of preparing food for consumption. It involves combining ingredients in various ways to create delicious meals.

Common Cooking Ingredients

Here are some essential ingredients used in cooking: 


Flour 
Sugar 
Salt 
Eggs 
Butter 
Milk

Popular Cooking Techniques 

Sautéing – Cooking food quickly in a small amount of fat. 
Boiling – Cooking food in water or broth at high temperatures. 
Baking – Cooking food using dry heat in an oven. 
Grilling – Cooking food over direct heat, usually on a grill.
